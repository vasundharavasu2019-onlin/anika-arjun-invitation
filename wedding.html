How We Met

A chance encounter at a cozy café in Bangalore. What started as a spilled latte turned into six hours of endless conversation. We knew something special had begun.

First Date

Dinner by the lake under a canopy of stars. We discovered our shared love for old Hindi songs and midnight chai. The night ended with promises of many more.

The Proposal

On a misty morning in Coorg, surrounded by coffee plantations and the Western Ghats, Arjun went down on one knee with a rose-gold ring. Through happy tears, she said yes.

Engagement

Celebrated with our closest family and friends, the engagement was a night of dance, laughter, and the sweet anticipation of forever. The journey to 'I do' officially began.
